How It Works

Ride in three
simple steps.

Roboride makes getting around NWA effortless. Open the app, request your Cybercab, and go. No tip required — not even a word.

01

Open the app & set your destination

Enter where you're going. The app shows your nearest Cybercab and arrival time in real time.

02

Your Cybercab arrives

A sleek Tesla Cybercab pulls up to your location. Confirm your ride via the app and step inside.

03

Sit back and arrive

Full autonomous navigation takes you to your destination safely. Track every mile on your phone.

Services

Built for every
NWA journey.

Everyday Rides

From Fayetteville neighborhoods to Bentonville's Crystal Bridges — get where you need to go quickly and affordably.

Corporate Accounts

Seamless employee transport across the Walmart and Tysons campuses. Centralized billing, custom pickup zones, full reporting.

Scheduled Rides

Book up to a week in advance. Early morning airport runs to XNA, Razorback games, late-night returns — always on time.

Event Shuttles

Coordinated transport for Walmart AMP concerts, Bikes Blues & BBQ, and Razorback games. Group bookings available.

Night Owl Mode

Safe, autonomous rides from Dickson Street, Bentonville Square, or Rogers 9 PM–4 AM, every night of the week.

Accessibility Rides

Wheelchair-accessible Cybercabs available on request. Low-step entry, ample cabin space, no driver interaction needed.
